Jonathan Wenger

I am a postdoctoral research scientist at Columbia University’s Zuckerman Institute working with John P. Cunningham. My research focuses on numerical methods for machine learning, in particular probabilistic numerics which interprets numerical methods as learning procedures.

Previously I was a doctoral student in computer science at the University of Tübingen and an IMPRS-IS fellow advised by Philipp Hennig.
